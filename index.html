Common Questions

We've heard every
concern. Here's the truth.

Is this really exclusive to multi-family properties?

Yes. ChargePark's program is purpose-built for apartment complexes, condominiums, townhome communities, and HOA-managed residential properties only. We do not serve retail, office, or commercial properties.

What does "zero investment" actually mean?

ChargePark covers 100% of hardware costs, electrical upgrades, permitting, installation, and ongoing maintenance. You pay absolutely nothing upfront or at any point over the 25-year term.

What if I want to sell the property?

The ChargePark agreement transfers to the new owner automatically. It adds value — buyers receive a built-in revenue stream and modern EV infrastructure from day one.

Who handles maintenance and repairs?

We do. Our team handles all hardware maintenance, software updates, and replacements. If a charger goes down, we fix it — at our expense, on our schedule.

How does billing and payment work?

Tenants pay ChargePark directly via app or card. We compile quarterly revenue, calculate your 50% share, and deposit directly to your account with a full report.

What size apartment complex qualifies?

We work with multi-family residential properties with 20 or more units — apartments, condominiums, mixed-use residential buildings, and gated communities with private parking.
